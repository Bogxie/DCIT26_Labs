Special Class Form

Special Job Information
  • Weapon: She continues to use a Hammer, but her skill set becomes much more refined and versatile.
  • Playstyle Shift: While the Holy Knight is about "holding the line," the Saint focuses on "Cleansing and Crushing." She gains more ways to remove debuffs from allies and deal impact damage to enemies.
  • Key Passive Upgrade: The Saint unlocks more slots and options for her Aura System, allowing her to switch between offensive and defensive modes more fluidly in the heat of battle.
  • Special Ability: She gains access to "Redemption," a powerful support mechanic that can save teammates from fatal blows or provide massive healing.
