Basic Form of Lime Serenity

First Job Information
  • Job Name: Holy Knight
  • Role: Primarily a defensive and support-oriented character.
  • Key Mechanic: Uses "Auras" to provide buffs to herself and teammates.
  • Weapon: A large heavy hammer (Maul).
  • Aura Limitations: She can only have three of the six available auras active, and they are divided between her job classes.
